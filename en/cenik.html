PRICE LIST

Prices are indicative and depend on the specific project, its scope and the duration of the collaboration. There is an extra charge for express delivery. Postage and travel expenses are charged separately and are not included in the listed prices. I am not a VAT payer. .

Download binding order form

Units and rates:

  • 1 standard page (SP) = 1,800 characters including spaces, calculated from the final (translated) text. The minimum charged amount is 1/2 SP.
  • Standardized documents (e.g. birth, marriage and death certificates, dregistry extracts) are charged per physical page (PP), due to complex formatting.
  • Interpreting is charged per hour. The minimum time charged is 15 minutes.
  • Wedding ceremonies are charged at a fixed rate per ceremony.
